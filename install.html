Installation Protocol

Official Distribution Guide v1.1

01

Secure Download

Click the "Download APK" button. If your browser displays a "File might be harmful" warning, select "Download anyway". This is standard for direct distribution.

02

System Authorization

Open the downloaded file. Go to Settings and enable "Allow from this source" for your browser. This enables secure self-updates.

03

Play Protect Override

If a Play Protect block appears, tap "More details" and then "Install anyway". Our binaries are signed and verified safe.
