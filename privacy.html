Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

The Warrior App ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Warrior App is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We do not collect personal identifiers, email addresses, or contact lists unless explicitly shared by you in the community section.

2. Use of Accessibility Services

IMPORTANT: Our application utilizes the Android AccessibilityService API to power the "Guardian Shield" feature.

How it is used: The Accessibility Service is used solely to detect user-defined keywords, application names, and URLs to prevent access to distracting or harmful content. This is essential for the core habit-breaking functionality of the app.

Data Privacy: We do NOT monitor, collect, or store any personal data through the Accessibility Service. No data captured by this service is ever transmitted to our servers or third parties. All processing happens locally and privately on your device.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your local data. Since your streak and habit data are stored locally, your privacy remains under your control.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Warrior App support channel.
