01. The Sovereign Protocol

NoRelapse operates as a sovereign vault. We believe that recovery data is the most sensitive information a human can generate.

Our architecture is designed to prevent data leakage at the hardware level. Every decision we make prioritizes your digital autonomy over our analytics.
